THE GREAT ENCOUNTER

In 2010, I was in a vision when I heard the voice of a lady/woman weeping and repeatedly saying; "Will you deliver me"? In 2011, a man of God by name Pastor Segun Obadje strangely called me out of the crowd as prophesied saying " thus says the Lord, settle down with the word of God, so that you will teach the from your spirit and not from your brain, thereby complimenting the body of Christ and not building another denomination. In 2013, I heard the Lord saying: " I have anointed you to preach the message of deliverance and the world will come and hear it from your mouth" In 2014, the Lord said " You should raise a gathering of believers and the name of the gathering should be called YESUNA .".... I latter discovered that means "MY JESUS" in Hausa. Oct 6, 2020 the Lord said He wants me to start a gathering of believers and He will heal the sick, raise the Dead and do things through us. He said He has prepared a property for us around Yakoyo central mosque. As such, our Geographical territory is ojodu Berger and it's environment.
